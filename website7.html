Your volunteer website

Volunteers need a place to call “home” on the web. You should build them a specific, branded location that provides them all the information they need to be part of your community. Most likely, this will be a website. We use an accessible Wordpress webste, but you can use any medium that you are familiar with.

What are some considerations for your developer zone?

  • Branded specifically for the volunteer effort of your organization
  • Clear instructions in how to get started
  • Links to GitHub Issues
  • Code of Conduct / Terms of Service
  • Links to the various departments and specific projects that you would like the developers to work on
  • Consider and introduction powerpoint or video
  • Your contact information
  • Knowledge area of tools and stacks your organization uses
